Roman Numeral Converter

Roman numerals are a numeral system that originated in ancient Rome and remained the usual way of writing numbers throughout Europe well into the Late Middle Ages. Since roman numerals doesn't include number 0 and has a limited potential of representing numbers, it is inefficient to do math. Yet it is widely used for artistic purposes.

Here you can find my converter app which converts an Arabic numeral in the range between 1 and 3999, to its equivalent Roman numeral.
